Security

We welcome vulnerability reports and will work with you in good faith to resolve issues.

Scope

  • In scope: kelh.net and subdomains owned by kelh.net.
  • Out of scope: third-party services not operated by kelh.net, social accounts, denial-of-service or resource exhaustion tests, physical or social engineering attacks.

How to report

  • File an issue: github.com/atiradonet/kelh.net/issues
  • Include: affected host/path, clear steps to reproduce, expected vs. actual behavior, impact, and any proof-of-concept. Screenshots or short logs are helpful.

Our commitment

  • Acknowledge reports within 30 business days.
  • Provide status updates at least every 30 business days until resolution.
  • Coordinate disclosure timelines with you.

Research guidelines

  • Avoid degrading service or accessing more data than necessary to demonstrate the issue.
  • No automated scanning that impacts availability.
  • Do not exfiltrate personal data; if accessed accidentally, stop, report, and delete it.

Safe harbor

If you make a good-faith effort to follow this policy, avoid privacy harms, and report promptly, we will not pursue legal action or ask law enforcement to investigate you for your research on in-scope assets.

Recognition / rewards

We don’t run a bounty program right now. When possible, we will offer thanks directly. No acknowledgments page is published at this time.

Cryptography

PGP/other encrypted channels are not yet available. If needed later, we’ll publish the key and note it in /.well-known/security.txt.
